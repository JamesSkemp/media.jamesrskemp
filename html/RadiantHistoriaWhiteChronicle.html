Chapter 1

The King's Daughter
During "The Bridge at Lazvil" node at Alistel, head to the tavern at the Second Ward. Climb the stairs to the left and in the upper left corner, talk to the girl, named Anna, to receive this sidequest. During the "An Ancient City" node at Granorg, head to the item shop at the Commercial District, and talk to the merchant there. During "The Resistance" node at Granorg, talk to Eruca. She can be found wearing a cloak, by a tree at the center of the Downtown area. Tell her to "Fulfill your duty". Return to the "To Granorg" node and talk to Anna to complete this sidequest and receive the RABBIT'S FOOT (MP+10/LUC+15). This sidequest is also a prerequisite for unlocking "The Show Goes On" part of the true ending.

Automatically completed during the plot.
Marco the Calm
During the "A New Mission" node at Alistel, on the right side of the first floor are two doors. Enter the left door and talk to the blue soldier to receive this sidequest. You need to find four scrolls for Marco. 1) Right-Cut Scroll: Reward from "Accident in the Cave" sidequest (see 036). 2) Left-Cut Scroll: Reward from "Despairing Young Man" sidequest (see 103). 3) Sleep Gas Scroll: During "To Cornet Village" at Cornet, Standard Chapter 3, (see CRNT1) check out the field to the right. Walk up between the upper row of crops and the fence to find a hidden chest which contains this scroll. 4) Roll-Cut Scroll: During "To Holff Ruins" at Abyssia Forest (see ABYS1), follow the upper path to a large, open area. Search the upper right corner for a hidden chest which contains this scroll. After you have obtained any of the scrolls, return to the soldier with them, and he will teach the new abilities to Marco. Learn all four abilities to complete this sidequest.

Red-Letter Day

At Journey's End
This one is very tricky, as it has prerequisite choices to even access the sidequest, and the game doesn't tell you. There are three decisions you have to make over the course of the game. 1) During the "Breakthrough" node at Gran Plain (see GRAN1), head north one screen and west to the checkpoint. You will hear someone in distress back east. Make sure to choose "Let's go save her now." 2) After the "Wolf in the Fold" node (see GRAN3), return to "Alistel's Offensive" and talk to Viola. Return to the "A Meeting Too Late" node and proceed with the plot normally. Make sure to choose "I leave it to you." 3) During the "Commander's Fall" node at the Sand Fortress (SAND6), defeat the Thaumaton boss at the end. Afterwards, make sure to choose "Bring back now." Despite that last decision occuring AFTER the time when you can access the sidequest, it is indeed a prerequisite. Return to the "Gafka & the Scrolls" node and head to Skalla.
